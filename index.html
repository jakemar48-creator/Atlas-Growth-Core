FAQ

Common Questions

How is Atlas Growth Core different from a marketing agency?

Traditional agencies focus on traffic. We focus on what happens after the click, the CRM, automation, AI, and systems that convert that traffic into booked patients and closed revenue. We only run ads once the back-end infrastructure is in place.

How quickly can the systems be live?

Core systems, including the AI receptionist, missed call text-back, and booking automation, are typically live within 7 to 14 days. Full CRM build-outs and ad campaigns take 2 to 4 weeks depending on scope and onboarding speed.

Do I need to already have a CRM?

No. We build your CRM from the ground up inside platforms like GoHighLevel or HubSpot, designed specifically for how your practice sells. If you already have a CRM, we'll audit and rebuild it to actually work.

What makes the AI agents different from a chatbot?

Standard chatbots follow scripts. Our AI agents handle natural conversations, qualify leads, answer clinical questions, book appointments, follow up via voice and SMS, and hand off warm leads directly to your team, all without human supervision.

Is there a setup fee?

All pricing shown is monthly retainer only. There is no separate setup fee. The build is included. We only win long-term if the system actually works, so we have every incentive to build it right from day one.

How do I know if my practice is ready for this?

If you're spending money on any form of marketing, such as Google Ads, social, or referrals, and you're not converting every lead into a booked appointment, you're ready. Book the free audit and we'll tell you exactly what's leaking and how to fix it.
